I am providing on set color grading and DIT work. I am using DaVinci Resolve on a Mac Pro Tower with a Red Rocket and two Nvidia graphics cards for realtime playback of Red, Alexa, Sony F65 and Phantom footage. I also provide either one light or color corrected Transcoding of these large format files to any codec required at fast speeds.

Most clients I work with receive all of their footage backed up onto two external hard drives as well as all of their footage Transcoded between 1-3 hours after I receive the last card or mag of the day. I have my own Color/DIT cart as well as a backup generator and a DIT tent to be totally self sufficient when needed. I also provide final color grading while on set for clients who do not have time to do a separate color grading session.

Recent projects I have worked on include Jennifer Lopez and Pit Bull's music video for the World Cup "We Are One", Belk clothing stores, Auto Nation with Zimmerman Advertising, Celebrity Cruiselines, Sandals Royal Bahamian and Grand Antigua resorts and Humana Healthcare with Lebron James.
